State of Debate is a social
network oriented, policy making, debate website.

In order to use it, you must be sent an email invitation by a friend.





What you do in State of Debate:










Become a member of your friend's tribe who invites you. And then, build your own tribe, by inviting your friends.








Start creating debates, opinioning with your arguments, and commenting on arguments.






Let loose with the rating cannon! Everything that you or anyone else submits to the public can be rated. But be forewarned, this ain't your Grandpa's Happy-Go-Lucky Facebook: there is a down vote.






When you think you're ready for the big leagues, you can start crafting and publishing solutions to debates. Put some thought into them, because there will be judgement.




From among the very highest rated chiefs of the tribes that make up State of Debate, a President will be elected, by the people, for the people. They will sit in office, pass legislation (AKA solutions) into law, with the help of their tribe (AKA Congress), and they will go down in history.
